By you: if you need to cancel, please tell us in writing as soon as possible. Charges are as follows:

  • Less than 30 days before the event — 100% of the total package price.

Rescheduling: we'll always try to accommodate a date change. One reschedule is offered free of charge when requested more than 30 days before the original date, subject to availability. Further changes, or requests made with less notice, may incur an admin fee and are subject to availability.

By us: in the rare event we have to cancel (e.g. illness, equipment failure beyond reasonable mitigation), we will, wherever possible, arrange a suitably skilled replacement photographer/videographer at no extra cost to you.
