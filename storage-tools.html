🧹 Orphaned File Scanner

Finds files sitting in Storage that aren't referenced anywhere in your database (usually leftover from uploads that were never saved, or deleted records that never cleaned up their file). Safe to review and delete.

🗜️ Compress Live Images

Scans every known image field (services, about page, homepage sections, campaigns, slides, avatars), downloads oversized images, compresses + resizes them for web, re-uploads, and updates the database automatically — so nothing on your live site breaks.
