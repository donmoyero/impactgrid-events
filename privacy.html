This Privacy Policy explains how ImpactGrid Group Ltd ("ImpactGrid", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you visit impactgridgroup.com or book our Services, in line with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. For the terms covering your booking itself, see our Terms & Conditions.

01Who We Are

ImpactGrid Group Ltd is the data controller responsible for your personal data. We're an events photography and videography studio based in Manchester, UK. You can reach us using the details in Section 13.

02Information We Collect

We collect information you give us directly, such as when you submit a booking request through our Book Us page:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Event type, date, guest count, and location/venue
  • Any notes or details you add about your event

We may also automatically collect limited technical information when you browse our site (e.g. browser type, pages visited) through cookies and similar technologies — see Section 5.

03How We Use It

We use your information to:

  • Review, confirm, and manage your booking
  • Communicate with you about your event (confirmations, invoices, reminders)
  • Keep records for accounting and tax purposes
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send you marketing about our services, where you've agreed to this or it's otherwise permitted by law — you can opt out at any time

Our legal basis for using your data is mainly to perform our contract with you (fulfilling your booking) and our legitimate business interests (e.g. record-keeping, improving our services). Where we rely on your consent — for example, marketing emails — you can withdraw it at any time.

04Photos, Video & Marketing

As explained in our Terms & Conditions, unless you tell us otherwise in writing before your event, we may use photos and video from your session in our portfolio, on our website, and across our social media for marketing purposes. If guests or other third parties appear in this content, please make sure they're aware a photographer/videographer will be present at your event.

05Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies, including:

  • Essential storage — used to remember your theme preference (light/dark mode) and keep the site working correctly
  • Meta (Facebook) Pixel — helps us understand how visitors use our site and shows relevant ads to people who've previously visited (retargeting)

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Blocking some cookies may affect how parts of the site work.

06Sharing Your Information

We don't sell your personal data. We only share it where necessary, with:

  • Service providers who help us run our booking system and store data securely (our booking platform and database providers)
  • Our authentication provider, used to securely manage admin access to our booking system
  • Meta (Facebook), for the advertising/analytics pixel described above
  • Professional advisors, such as our accountant, where required
  • Law enforcement or regulators, where we're legally required to

07International Transfers

Some of our service providers may store or process data outside the UK. Where this happens, we make sure appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) are in place to protect your data to UK GDPR standards.

08How Long We Keep Your Data

We keep booking and client data for as long as needed to deliver our services, and afterwards for up to 6 years to meet our accounting and tax obligations, unless we're required to keep it for longer (for example, a legal dispute). Photos and video may be retained for longer where used in our portfolio, unless you ask us to remove them.

09Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Ask us to delete your data, in certain circumstances
  • Object to, or ask us to restrict, how we use your data
  • Ask for your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where we rely on it

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 13.

10Keeping Your Data Secure

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. No online system is ever 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to keep your information safe.

11Children

Our Services are intended for adults booking events. We don't knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

12Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "last updated" date.
