Our Story

Impact Grid Events was founded after Dara identified a growing problem in the event industry — great events were often followed by poor photography quality, slow media delivery, and a poor attendee experience after the event ended.

To solve this, he brought together TJ, a creative focused on delivering high-quality photography and memorable content creation, and Dara, who develops the technology and digital systems behind the experience.

Together, the team built Impact Grid Events to combine photography, content creation, and technology into one seamless event media platform.
